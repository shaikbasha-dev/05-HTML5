# Applications of HTML5

## Overview

HTML5 is the foundation of modern web development and is used across a wide range of industries to create websites, web applications, and interactive digital experiences. Its support for semantic elements, multimedia, graphics, responsive design, and modern browser APIs makes it suitable for both simple web pages and large-scale enterprise applications.

Today, almost every website on the Internet uses HTML5 as its structural framework.

---

# Major Applications of HTML5

## 1. Business Websites

HTML5 is widely used to develop professional business websites that showcase products, services, company information, and contact details.

Examples:

- Corporate websites
- Company portfolios
- Service-based websites
- Startup landing pages

Benefits:

- Professional structure
- Responsive layouts
- Better SEO
- Improved accessibility
