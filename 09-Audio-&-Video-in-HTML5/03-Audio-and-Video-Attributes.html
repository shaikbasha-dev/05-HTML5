HTML5 Audio and Video Attributes Example

This example demonstrates commonly used attributes of the HTML5 <audio> and <video> elements.

Audio Example

Audio Attributes Used

  • controls - Displays playback controls.
  • loop - Repeats the audio automatically.
  • preload="metadata" - Loads only audio metadata.

Video Example

Video Attributes Used

  • controls - Displays playback controls.
  • width - Sets the video width.
  • height - Sets the video height.
  • poster - Displays a thumbnail before playback.
  • preload="metadata" - Loads only video metadata.
  • muted - Starts the video without sound.

Summary

HTML5 provides several useful attributes for controlling the behavior and appearance of audio and video elements. These attributes improve usability, accessibility, and performance while delivering a better multimedia experience.
